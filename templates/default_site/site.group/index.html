Welcome. I'm a hybrid web designer/developer and a bit of a font nerd. I design clean, usable, and fun interfaces and then do the front-end development to bring them to life. I can talk to both web designers and developers in their native languages, helping both to understand the constraints and challenges the other faces. I love good design and want to see more of it in the world!
